What is Stock Station?

Stock Station is a web application enabling users or budding traders to regularly invest into trending shares / stocks / equities using altcoins (virtual currency). The major functionalities of this web application include showing real time prices of trending shares, variation in share prices, regular update of user portfolio and display current insights about the stock market.

Why Stock Station?

OUR PURPOSE

Stock Station” involves altcoins which is an alternate to legal tender / legal currency.
